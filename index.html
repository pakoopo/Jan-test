Renewable Energy Sources

Renewable energy sources are energy sources that are replenished naturally and are sustainable over the long term. They include:

  • Solar Energy
  • Wind Energy
  • Hydropower
  • Biomass Energy
  • Geothermal Energy

These energy sources are crucial for reducing greenhouse gas emissions and combating climate change.
